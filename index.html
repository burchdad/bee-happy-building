Build Process

A Clearer Way to Build

From the first property conversation to the final walkthrough, every phase should feel informed, organized, and intentional.

  1. 01Consultation

    Property goals, lifestyle, timeline, budget, land, utilities, and must-have spaces.

  2. 02Site Visit

    Access, drainage, utilities, approach, views, and the realities that shape a better build.

  3. 03Planning

    Layout, scope, materials, budget priorities, and schedule expectations become practical.

  4. 04Engineering

    Drawings, structure, code needs, documentation, and pre-construction decisions align.

  5. 05Construction

    Sequenced work, craft, updates, and respect for the property Bee Happy is trusted to improve.

  6. 06Warranty

    The walkthrough and handoff make the finished building feel documented and complete.
