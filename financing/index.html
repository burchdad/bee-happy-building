Financing

Prepare for better lender conversations.

Project financing depends on land, scope, use, finish level, and lender requirements. Start with organized project information.

01

Land Status

Owned land, pending purchase, acreage, utilities, access, and survey information can affect financing conversations.

Scope & Budget

Documents

Plans, estimates, property information, and project details help lenders understand what is being built.

What To Gather

  • Property address or county
  • Land ownership status
  • Desired building type and square footage
  • Target budget range
  • Preferred timeline
  • Any inspiration images or plan references
